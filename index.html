About me

I am a self/community taught web developer with freelance experience building a website for the East Tennessee Relic & Coin Hunters Club. After spending my 20s working various fast food and factory jobs that didn't suit me and didn't challenge me I have finally found what I am supposed to be doing.

Why I enjoy web development

The pride and fulfillment I feel creating something for people that is functional, fun to use and nice to look at. The challenge of problem-solving and the accomplishment of solving it is what I have always wanted from the professional side of my life.

Technologies I know

HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6), Git, Bootstrap, Netlify
